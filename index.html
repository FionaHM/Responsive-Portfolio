About Me

Welcome! You are about to view my Web Development Portfolio, but first let me introduce myself.


I am originally from Ireland and left in 2000 after completing my Masters Degree. I hold a Bachelor of Engineering and a Master of Management Science from the National University of Ireland, UCD, and a Bachelor of Science in Cloud Computing from Cork Regional Technical College, Ireland. I have spent the past 16 years both living and working around the world - London, Singapore, Dublin, Vilnius and Chicago, where I am currently based.


I have recently moved into Web Development, having spent most of my career working in IT Production, in both Service Delivery and IT Projects. I started my career as an IT Management Consultant with PricewaterhouseCoopers in London, after which I moved into the Investment Banking domain, where I worked for UBS in both London and Singapore.


Now that you know a bit about me, please use the links at the top of the page to view my portfolio and contact me. I hope to hear from you in the future!
